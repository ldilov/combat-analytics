2. **Pillar Erasure:** Consolidating 30 reason-codes into 4 pillars is a catastrophic loss of resolution. "Consistency" is a meaningless word when the actual problem is "missed 2nd interrupt on 3rd cast." You’ve traded specific, actionable prescriptions for vague "scores." You're giving them a GPA when they need a corrected essay.
3. **The New Player Void:** This design assumes a baseline of mastery. A player with <5 sessions doesn't have a "personal baseline" or "matchup mastery delta." They get a screen full of N/A values and "unknown" nodes. It’s an alienating experience for the very people who need the most guidance.
